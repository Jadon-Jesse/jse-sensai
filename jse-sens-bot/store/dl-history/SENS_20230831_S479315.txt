          SUMMARISED GROUP UNAUDITED INTERIM FINANCIAL STATEMENTS FOR THE SIX MONTHS
          ENDED 30 JUNE 2023                                                      
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
          1. SALIENT FEATURES                                                     
                                                                                  
          Operating loss increased by 26.14% to R16.5 million, from R13.1 million in the prior corresponding
          period.                                                                 
          Headline loss per share (“HEPS”) decreased by 11.44% to 16.61 cents per share, from 14.90 cents
          per share in the prior corresponding period.                            
                                                                                  
          Loss per share (“EPS”) decreased by 11.44% to 16.61 cents per share, from 14.90 cents per share in
          the prior corresponding period.                                         
                                                                                  
          Net asset value per share (“NAVPS”) decreased by 19.40% to 105.39 cents per share, from 130.76
          cents per share in the prior corresponding period.

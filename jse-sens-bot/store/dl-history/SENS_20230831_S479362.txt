        TRUSTCO: CLOSED PERIOD SHARE REPURCHASE  PROGRAMME                        
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
        Shareholders are advised that the Company has, in compliance with paragraph 5.72(h) of
        the Listings Requirements of the JSE Limited ("JSE"), established a share repurchase
                                                                                  
        programme. In terms of the repurchase programme, the Company, either directly or through
        a subsidiary company, may repurchase ordinary shares during its closed period
                                                                                  
        ("Repurchase Programme").                                                 
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
        The maximum number of shares that can be repurchased in terms of the Repurchase
                                                                                  
        Programme will be 197 447 716 ordinary shares if acquired by Trustco and 98 723 858 if
        acquired by a subsidiary of the Company.                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
        This closed period commences on 1 September 2023 and terminates on or about 30
        November 2023, or the date of publication of its financial statements, whichever period is
                                                                                  
        the longest (the “Prohibited Period” or “Repurchase Programme Period”).
